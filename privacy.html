Privacy Policy

Application: Algo Desk Monitor · Last updated: 21 July 2026

Algo Desk Monitor ("the App") is a private, personal analytics tool operated by Ibe Nwandu. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled. The App is not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance.

1. What data the App accesses

After you authorize it through TikTok's OAuth consent screen, the App reads, from your own TikTok account only, via the TikTok Display API:

  • Basic profile information (user.info.basic) — open ID, display name, avatar.
  • Account statistics (user.info.stats) — follower, following, likes, and video counts.
  • Your own videos and their public metrics (video.list) — view, like, comment, and share counts, plus title, description, and cover.

The App reads this data only. It never accesses other users' data, and it does not post, edit, or delete anything on TikTok.

2. How the data is used

The metrics are used solely to compute content-performance analytics for the operator (e.g. which posts hold attention or get shared). Data is stored in a local, private database on the operator's own machine and is not published.

3. Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. No advertising or tracking is involved.

4. Retention and deletion

Metric snapshots are retained locally to compute trends over time. You can revoke the App's access at any time in your TikTok settings (Security & login → Manage app permissions), which stops all further data access. To have any stored data deleted, contact the address below.

5. Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: ibenwandu@gmail.com.
